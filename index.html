Welcome to my text based Choose Your Own Adventure Game

Dungeon of the abyss

You are a brave adventurer whom has ventured forth to enter into the dungeon of the abyss to plunder the riches within

You start in the middle of the dungeon there are four choices.

  • Open the old wooden door in front of you and venture forth
  • Pull the suspicious lever on the wall to your right
  • Crawl down the dark creepy tunnel behind you
  • Carefully cross the broken bridge to your left
